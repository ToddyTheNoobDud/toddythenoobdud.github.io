Privacy Policy

Learn how Kenium Music Bot handles your data

Last Updated: June 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Kenium Music Bot collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Discord music bot services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

Kenium Music Bot collects minimal data necessary to provide music services:

Guild IDs

Stored for 24/7 functionality when the bot is actively used in your server

Text Channel IDs

Stored for 24/7 functionality to remember where music commands are used

Voice Channel IDs

Stored for 24/7 functionality to maintain music playback sessions

Username & User ID

Used for playlist parsing and management features

Why We Need This Data

Music Service Functionality

The collected data is essential for providing continuous music services, managing playlists, and ensuring the bot can reconnect to voice channels and remember your music preferences across sessions.

Specific Uses:

Guild, Channel, and User IDs: Required for our system to identify where to play music, which users have created playlists, and to maintain 24/7 music functionality.

Command Data: When a music command is used, we may temporarily store command details for up to 14 days for debugging and service improvement purposes.

Data Sharing

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively for providing music bot services and is kept secure within our systems.

Data Removal

You have the right to request removal of your data at any time. Response times may vary and could take up to 7 days to process your request completely.

To request data deletion or if you have questions about your data, please contact us through our support channels listed below.

Note: You can also delete your own data, such as playlists, by using the appropriate commands within the bot. For example, use the command /playlist delete to remove your playlists.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Continued use of Kenium Music Bot after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
